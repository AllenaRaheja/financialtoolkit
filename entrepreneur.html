Entrepreneur Costs

Business Registration

Cost to register a business: $100 - $300.

Legal Fees

Legal consultation and paperwork: $1,000 - $3,000.

Office Space

Rent for office space: $500 - $2,000 per month.

Manufacturing Costs

Purchase of necessary equipment to start the buissness initally: around $50,000

Marketing

Initial marketing and branding expenses: $500 - $2,000. (not a hardcore marketing strategy)

Insurance

Business insurance coverage: $300 - $1,000 annually.
