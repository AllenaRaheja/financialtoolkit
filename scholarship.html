Need-Based Scholarships

Need-based scholarships are granted based on your financial need. These scholarships are designed to help students who require financial assistance to pursue their education.

Example: The Canada Student Grants provide up to $3,000 per year to students from low- and middle-income families.

"Need-based scholarships provide essential support to students who might otherwise be unable to afford higher education." - ScholarshipAmerica.org

Merit-Based Scholarships

Merit-based scholarships are awarded based on your achievements in various fields such as academics, sports, or arts. These scholarships aim to reward excellence.

Example: The Loran Award offers up to $100,000 over four years to high-achieving students who demonstrate leadership potential and commitment to service.

"Merit-based scholarships recognize and celebrate outstanding students who excel in their academic and extracurricular endeavors." - Loran Scholars Foundation

Specialized Scholarships

Specialized scholarships are offered for specific fields of study or career goals. These scholarships support students pursuing particular majors or professions.

Example: The Canadian Medical Hall of Fame Award supports students pursuing a career in medicine with a $5,000 scholarship.

"Specialized scholarships encourage students to pursue their passions and contribute to specific fields of study." - CMHF.org
